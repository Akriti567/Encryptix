About Us

Espresso Express

Espresso Express was founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiasts who were passionate about bringing high-quality coffee to their community.Espresso Express is a cozy coffee shop located in Basantapur. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee greets customers as they step inside, and the friendly staff welcomes them with a smile. The shop is known for its exceptional espresso-based drinks, which are made with locally roasted beans and served with artistic latte art.The interior of Espresso Express is warm and inviting, with rustic wooden tables and chairs, exposed brick walls, and vintage decor. The shop offers free Wi-Fi, making it a popular spot for students and professionals to work or study while enjoying their coffee. Since then, we have grown to become one of the most popular coffee shops in the area, known for our delicious coffee and friendly atmosphere.Our mission is to provide our customers with an exceptional coffee experience every time they visit us. We source our beans from the best coffee growers around the world and roast them in-house to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

Types Of Coffee

  • 1. Americano
  • 2. Macchiato
  • 3. Espresso
  • 4. Double Espresso
  • 5. Mocha
  • 6. Iced Coffee
  • Americano - A type of coffee made by adding hot water to espresso.
  • Macchiato - A type of coffee that is prepared by adding a small amount of milk to an espresso shot.
  • Espresso - A concentrated form of coffee made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans.
  • Double Espresso - A stronger version of espresso made by using twice the amount of coffee and water.
  • Mocha - A type of coffee that is made with espresso, milk, and chocolate syrup or powder.
  • Iced Coffee - A cold version of coffee that can be prepared by brewing hot coffee and then cooling it, or by using cold brewusthods.
